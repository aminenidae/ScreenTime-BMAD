# Facebook Group Strategy

Facebook is different — you can't just post promotional content. The approach is **value-first in existing communities**.

## Target Groups to Join:
- "Homeschooling Moms" (1M+ members)
- "Screen Time Parents" or similar
- "Raising Readers" parent groups
- "Kids and Technology" groups
- "Positive Parenting" communities

## Engagement-First Approach (Do This Weekly):

**Week 1**: Answer screen time questions in threads. Don't mention the app. Just help.

**Week 2**: Post a helpful tip (use content from the "Parent Tips" pillar). Soft mention: "I'm actually building an app around this concept."

**Week 3**: Ask a question: "Parents — what's your current screen time system? I'm doing research." Read all answers.

**Week 4**: "Soft launch" share: "I've been sharing tips here for a few weeks. My app just went through Apple review — it's called Brain Coinz and it automates the system I've been describing. Would love feedback from this group when it launches."

**Rule**: Never post promotional content cold. Give value first, always.
