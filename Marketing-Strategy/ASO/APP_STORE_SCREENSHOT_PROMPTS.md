# App Store Screenshot AI Editing Prompts

## Global Style Guidelines (Apply to All)

**Device Frame:** iPhone 15 Pro, Natural Titanium frame
**Text Font:** Bold sans-serif (SF Pro Display or similar)
**Headline Position:** Top 20% of canvas
**Device Position:** Centered, bottom-aligned, ~75% of canvas height
**Output Size:** 1290 x 2796 pixels (6.7" display)

---

## Screenshot 1: THE HOOK

**Headline:** `REWARD Screen Time, Don't Restrict It`

**Prompt:**
```
Create an App Store marketing screenshot with:

LAYOUT:
- Solid teal-to-dark-teal gradient background (#00A6A6 to #007A7A)
- Headline "REWARD Screen Time, Don't Restrict It" in bold white text at top
- "REWARD" should be slightly larger/bolder than the rest
- iPhone 15 Pro device frame (natural titanium) centered below headline
- Device shows the provided app screenshot
- Subtle yellow accent glow behind the device frame

STYLE:
- Clean, modern, premium feel
- High contrast white text on teal
- Slight drop shadow on device frame
- No additional decorative elements - let the UI speak

EMPHASIS:
- The circular time balance ring in the app should be the visual focal point
```
