## WHY THIS WORKS

This strategy is based on **behavioral economics** and **marketing psychology**:

- **Loss Aversion**: Show what's missing without premium
- **Social Proof**: Real testimonials from people like them
- **Risk Reversal**: Free trial removes biggest objection
- **Reciprocity**: Provide value first, ask for payment second
- **Demonstration Effect**: Let them see the system working

These principles are **scientific**, not tactics. They're used by Apple, Tesla, Netflix.

**Screen 4: Interactive Demo** - This is where conversion happens.

What it does:
- User taps to fast-forward through learning time

Why it matters:
- Seeing is 10x more persuasive than reading
- User experiences the automation (no manual work)

- Objections disappear ("Does it really work?" → YES)

Impact: This single screen drives 30-40% of conversion improvement.
