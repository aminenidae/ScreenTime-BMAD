## SCREEN 1: THE PROBLEM RECOGNITION
### "The Daily Battle Can End Today"

**Screen Type**: Animated Illustration + Copy
**Duration**: ~20-30 seconds (auto-advance or button)
**Conversion Target**: 85% proceed

**Key Elements**:
- **Headline**: Problem statement, not feature
- **Animation**: Show frustration escalating (relatable)
- **Copy**: Short, conversational, first-person perspective
- **CTA**: Action verb ("See How It Works")
- **Escape routes**: Skip option (don't force engagement)

```

There's a better way.
```
