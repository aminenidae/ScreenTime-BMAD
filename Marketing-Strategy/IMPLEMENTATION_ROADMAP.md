# ScreenTime-BMAD: Marketing-First Onboarding Implementation Roadmap
## From Strategy to Reality - 8-Week Plan

**Prepared**: December 14, 2025
**Status**: Ready for Execution
**Estimated Timeline**: 8 weeks from kickoff to launch
**Team Size**: 2-4 people

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### What We're Building
A marketing-first onboarding flow that converts 3-5x more users to paying subscribers by:
1. Teaching parents about the unique value proposition
2. Showing the system working (interactive demo)
3. Building urgency through social proof
4. Making the paywall feel natural and low-risk

### Expected Results
- **Current baseline**: 3-5% of users subscribe after onboarding
- **After implementation**: 25-30% of users subscribe (trial conversion)
- **Timeline to revenue impact**: 6 weeks

### Key Changes from Current
- ✅ 2 new awareness screens (problem + solution)
- ✅ 1 interactive demo screen (most important)
- ✅ 1 social proof screen
- ✅ Rewritten copy on all screens
- ✅ New animation/interaction requirements
- ✅ Better paywall positioning and messaging
