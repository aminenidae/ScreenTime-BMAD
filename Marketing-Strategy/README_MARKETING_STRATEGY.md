## üéì WHAT YOU'RE IMPLEMENTING

This isn't just a UI redesign. You're implementing **behavioral economics and marketing psychology** into your product experience.

Specifically:

1. **Loss Aversion**: Show what they're missing in free version
2. **Social Proof**: Testimonials from people like them
3. **Anchoring**: "$4.99/month" framed as "less than a coffee"
4. **Risk Reversal**: Free trial with "cancel anytime"
5. **Reciprocity**: After providing value, asking for payment feels fair
6. **Storytelling**: Taking parents on a journey, not just selling
7. **Demonstration Effect**: Showing > telling (Screen 4)

These principles are **more powerful than discounts or dark patterns**. And they feel good to the user.
