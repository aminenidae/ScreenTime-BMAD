## App Description (4000 chars max)

```
Stop the daily battles over screen time. ScreenTime Rewards transforms device management from conflict to collaboration. Children earn entertainment time by completing educational activities in apps you define.

THE TIME BANK DIFFERENCE

Unlike traditional blockers that create resentment, ScreenTime Rewards uses a "Time Bank" system. Learning time automatically converts to reward minutes that roll over - no daily reset, no lost progress. When your child earns time, their favorite apps unlock instantly without waiting for parent approval.

WHAT PARENTS LOVE

- Zero-friction rewards: Earned time unlocks apps automatically
- You define "learning": Choose any apps that count as educational
- Real-time dashboard: Monitor progress from your own device via iCloud
- Time rollover: Banked minutes carry forward, teaching saving habits
- Per-app tracking: See exactly where time is spent

MOTIVATION THAT WORKS

Children stay engaged through streak tracking and achievement badges. Daily streaks earn bonus time, turning consistency into a game. Watch your child go from resistant to enthusiastic about learning time.

PRIVACY-FIRST DESIGN

All data stays within your Apple ecosystem. We use Apple's official Screen Time APIs and sync through your private iCloud - no third-party servers, no advertising, no data selling. Your family's information belongs to your family.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Parents select Learning Apps and Reward Apps
2. Child uses learning apps to earn time
3. Reward apps automatically unlock when balance is positive
4. Monitor everything from your parent dashboard

SUBSCRIPTION OPTIONS

- Individual: $7.99/month (1 child)
- Family: $12.99/month or $59.99/year (up to 5 children)
- 30-day free trial included

Requires iOS 16.6 or later. Screen Time authorization needed for full functionality.

Transform screen time from a source of conflict into a tool for growth. Download ScreenTime Rewards today.
```
